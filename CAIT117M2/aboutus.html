Located in Kensington, New York, Arrowsmith Burgers, is one of the city’s premier burger restaurants. With 27 different kinds of burgers and over 2000 sandwich varieties, our customers never get bored. Included in our 27 varieties is 5 kinds of fish burgers and 3 different veggie burgers.

In addition to burgers, we offer a wide array of fresh salads, fries, and other side dishes. We have a selection of over 50 premium red and white wines for the more sophisticated diner. We also offer a special kid’s menu with reduced prices.

In addition to our food, we also pay special attention to the details of our accommodations. Our wide floor space and high ceilings gives our customers room to enjoy their food in an open and comfortable atmosphere. Our mission is to provide a quality dining experience for all of our customers. We accomplish this by providing quality food, in quality conditionsn at affordable prices.
